Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers (halved and deseeded)
  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 for higher fat)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or beef tallow
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese (adds fat and richness)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar or mozzarella
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh herbs (basil, oregano, thyme—your choice)
  • Optional: chopped olives, jalapeños, or mushrooms for variety
  • Cover with foil for the first 30–35 minutes to keep peppers from drying out, then uncover to brown the tops. Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix raw beef with spices, herbs, and any add-ins (cheese, cream cheese, etc.).
  3. Spoon the mixture into pepper halves and place in a baking dish.
  4. Bake covered for 40-50 minutes, or until internal meat temp hits 160°F.
  5. Serve with fresh herbs on top.
